Favorite Game's

Elden Ring

"Elden Ring" is an upcoming action role-playing game developed by FromSoftware, known for their critically acclaimed titles like the "Dark Souls" series, "Bloodborne," and "Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice." The game is a collaboration between FromSoftware and fantasy novelist George R.R. Martin, the author of "A Song of Ice and Fire" series, which inspired the hit TV show "Game of Thrones."

Favorite Anime's

One Piece

"One Piece" follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy, a young pirate with the ability to stretch his body like rubber after eating a supernatural fruit. Luffy and his crew, known as the Straw Hat Pirates, travel the vast ocean in search of the ultimate treasure called the "One Piece." Luffy's goal is to become the Pirate King and find the One Piece, which will grant him great power and prestige.
